What Is This?

This is a tool for reading International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) notation aloud.

It makes it easy to actually hear how words are pronounced based on their phonetic spelling, without having to look up each character in the IPA chart and string the sounds together yourself.

For example, "Conversation" can be pronounced according to its IPA notation /ˌkɒn.Vəˈseɪ.Ʃən/.


How Do You Use It?

Find a word you would like to hear pronounced, grab the IPA notation
